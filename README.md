# E-commerce Delivery & Customer Behavior Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes Target’s e-commerce operations in Brazil using a dataset containing 100,000+ orders placed between 2016 and 2018.

The analysis focuses on:
- Customer behavior
- Order trends
- Payment preferences
- Freight and logistics
- Delivery performance
- State-wise business insights

The goal is to uncover actionable business insights and provide recommendations to improve operational efficiency and customer experience.

---

## Business Problem
As a Data Analyst/Data Scientist, the objective was to analyze Target’s e-commerce dataset and extract meaningful insights related to:
- Customer distribution
- Sales growth
- Seasonal trends
- Payment behavior
- Delivery timelines
- Freight performance

---

## Key Insights
- Strong order growth from 2016–2018
- São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Minas Gerais generated the highest order volumes
- Orders peaked between February and August
- Most customers placed orders during the afternoon
- Credit cards were the dominant payment method
- Many deliveries arrived earlier than estimated

---

## Business Recommendations
- Expand logistics and inventory in high-demand states
- Improve targeted marketing in top-performing regions
- Schedule promotions during peak customer activity hours
- Optimize freight costs in high-order regions
- Offer loyalty rewards for frequent credit-card and UPI users
